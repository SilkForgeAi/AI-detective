Ethical Guidelines for AI Detective

CRITICAL DISCLAIMER

This tool is for research and educational purposes only. It is NOT for vigilante work, harassment, or taking the law into your own hands.

See DISCLAIMER.md for complete legal and ethical restrictions.

Core Principles

AI Detective is designed to assist human investigators, not replace them. All AI-generated insights are suggestions requiring human verification.

Privacy and Data Protection

Public Data Only
Only use publicly available data sources
Never access restricted or classified information
Flag attempts to input non-public data

Anonymization
Automatically anonymize personal information
Redact sensitive data (SSNs, phone numbers, emails)
Provide options for additional privacy controls

Compliance
Adhere to GDPR, CCPA, and FOIA guidelines
Warn users about legal risks
Maintain audit trails for accountability

Bias Mitigation

Fairness
Use bias detection frameworks
Test models for demographic fairness
Provide explainable AI outputs

Transparency
Show confidence scores for all findings
Explain how conclusions were reached
Allow users to review AI reasoning

Human-in-the-Loop

Verification Required
High-stakes outputs require human verification
Position AI as assistant, not decision-maker
Never make definitive claims without evidence

Error Handling
Flag low-confidence results
Warn about potential hallucinations
Provide fallback mechanisms

Harm Prevention

Sensitivity
Use appropriate language for sensitive topics
Avoid retraumatizing families
Provide content warnings when needed

Accountability
Maintain audit trails
Log all analyses for review
Support oversight by authorities

Usage Guidelines

Do:
Use for legitimate cold case investigations
Verify all AI findings with original evidence
Respect privacy and confidentiality
Follow legal and ethical guidelines
Report any new information to local authorities immediately

Don't:
Use for vigilante actions
Make definitive claims without verification
Access restricted data
Violate privacy laws
Use for harassment or stalking
Take matters into your own hands - always report to law enforcement
